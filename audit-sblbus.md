# Website Audit — sblbus.com
**Shuttle Bus Leasing | Used Buses for Lease**
Platform: WordPress + Divi 4.27.6 + WooCommerce | Sister company to CCW/TSI | Riverside, CA
Audit Date: 2026-03-17

---

## Overall Score: 41/100 *(Lowest of the three)*

---

## Critical Issues

1. **Same Divi crawlability problem** — homepage has zero semantic HTML. Invisible to Google.
2. **No meta description** — confirmed missing.
3. **Blog last updated October 2023** — 17 months stale. Google's freshness algorithm treats this as a dormant business.
4. **No pricing on inventory** — every listing forces a "Request Information" phone call. Buyers doing preliminary research move on to competitors who show price ranges.
5. **Copyright says © 2022** — it's 2026. Minor detail, major trust signal to first-time visitors.
6. **No lead capture on homepage** — no form, no phone in the hero, no "Get a Lease Quote" CTA. Every visitor who bounces is gone permanently.
7. **"Prison Transports" in main nav** — sitting alongside transit agency products. Brand perception issue for the primary buyer persona.
8. **WooCommerce cart/checkout pages are live and indexable** — dead infrastructure wasting Google's crawl budget.

---

## Revenue Impact

One transit agency on a multi-year lease is worth $50K–$500K. SBL ranks for essentially no lease-specific search terms ("short term bus lease," "gap fleet leasing transit," "seasonal shuttle bus lease"). Zero lead capture means zero follow-up on every visitor who doesn't pick up the phone.

---

## Automation Opportunities

- Lease inquiry intake → auto-route to sales rep → confirmation + PDF overview of available inventory → 3-day and 7-day follow-up sequence
- Inventory availability alerts — "notify me when a 40ft low-floor becomes available"
- Fleet gap analysis calculator (emergency rehab coverage) with lead capture
- Cross-sell routing to TSI (purchase) or CCW (rehab) when lease isn't the right fit

---

## Top 5 Fixes (Priority Order)

1. Add phone number and "Get a Quote" CTA to every page header — 30-minute fix
2. Update copyright year (2022 → 2026) across all three sites
3. Add price ranges ("starting at") to inventory listings — removes the friction killing conversion
4. Publish 2 blog posts/month targeting lease-specific keywords — compounding ROI within 60–90 days
5. Add Organization schema with consistent NAP
