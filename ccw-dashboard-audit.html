Security Headers Missing

Commercial
Browser security warnings on Chrome/Firefox scare away commercial buyers doing preliminary research. Insurance and enterprise procurement teams run security scans before signing contracts.

ADA Title III lawsuits cost $25K–$100K in settlements. Screen reader users — including many fleet managers with disabilities — can't use the site at all. Google penalizes accessibility failures in rankings.

Commercial
Large transit agencies and fleet operators (commercial) use the same research process as government buyers. No credentials page means no proof of scale or capability — they call a competitor who has it.

EO 14028 + NIST SP 800-161 — Unvetted third-party scripts signal inadequate supply chain security posture. CMMC Level 2 SR.3.169 makes this a formal audit finding.
Commercial
Unaudited scripts slow page load (every plugin adds 80–300ms). Page speed is a direct Google ranking factor. Commercial buyers on slow mobile connections bounce before they call.

Commercial
Transit agency procurement managers doing preliminary vendor research for large fleet purchases ($500K+) require evidence of comparable scope. No portfolio = no call back. Same psychology, different regulatory name.

Commercial
Unlabeled form fields confuse all users, not just screen reader users. Higher form abandonment rate on RFQ/contact forms = fewer leads captured from the traffic you're already getting.
